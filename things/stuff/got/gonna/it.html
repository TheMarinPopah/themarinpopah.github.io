How to get any extension on your Chromebook:

First, click the download button.
Second, open your Files app by clicking the circle button on the bottom left corner of your screen and click recent.
Third, double click extension.
Fourth, go back to the files app and click my files. Then double click downloads.
Fifth, click extension.zip with two fingers touching the mouspad.
Sixth, click extract all. It should be one of the options on the menu.

Seventh, go to chrome://extensions by pasting the exact link in your browser.
Eighth, turn on developer mode on the top right corner of the screen.
Ninth, click the Load Unpacked button on the top left of the page.
Tenth, double click My Files. Then double click Downloads.
Eleventh, click the top file ONE TIME. This should be a folder called Extension(1).
Finally, click open.
You should now have an extension opened up on your Chromebook.
